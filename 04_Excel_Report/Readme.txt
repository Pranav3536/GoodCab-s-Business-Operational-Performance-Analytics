### GoodCabs: Excel Reports for Business Analysis

This folder contains Excel reports generated for GoodCabs business analysis. 

1. City-wise Business Performance
   - Description: Overview of trips, revenue, and fares per city
   - Metrics: City, Total Trips, Total Revenue, Avg Fare per Trip, Revenue Contribution %

2. Weekday vs Weekend Performance
   - Description: Compare revenue and trips for weekdays vs weekends per city
   - Metrics: City, Total Trips, Avg Fare, Revenue Share

3. Passenger Loyalty Analysis
   - Description: Analyze new vs repeat passengers and loyalty rates
   - Metrics: City, New Passengers, Repeat Passengers, Total Passengers, Repeat Rate %
