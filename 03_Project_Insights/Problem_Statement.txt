📌 PROBLEM STATEMENT

GoodCabs is a growing cab service company operating in tier-2 cities across India. As the business 
expands, the company generates a large amount of operational data related to trips, revenue, 
passenger ratings, and city performance.

However, this data is scattered and difficult to interpret without proper analysis. Decision makers do 
not have a unified view of important business metrics such as trip demand trends, revenue contribution 
by city, passenger satisfaction, and operational efficiency.

Without structured analysis, it becomes challenging for the operations team to identify high-
performing cities, detect demand patterns, and evaluate whether business targets are being achieved.

Therefore, this project focuses on analyzing transportation data and building an interactive Power BI 
dashboard that helps stakeholders monitor key metrics and support data-driven decision making.
