==================================================
      GOODCABS END-TO-END ANALYTICS DASHBOARD
==================================================

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of transportation operations data, focusing on demand patterns, passenger behavior, and performance against targets.

The dashboard is divided into four key sections:

1. Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of total trips, total revenue, average fare per km, and passenger ratings. It also highlights top and bottom performing cities along with monthly trends.

2. Demand Analysis
Analyzes ride demand across weekdays and weekends, identifies peak demand days and months, and compares city-wise trip distribution.

3. Passenger Analysis
Focuses on customer behavior including total passengers, repeat passenger rate, passenger segmentation (new vs repeat), and retention trends.

4. Target Performance
Compares actual performance with predefined targets, highlights gaps in trips and ratings, and provides city-level performance status.

Key Highlights:
- Weekday demand is significantly higher than weekend demand
- Monday is the peak demand day
- Jaipur is the top-performing city in terms of trips
- Repeat passenger rate is relatively low, indicating scope for improving retention
- Some cities are underperforming against targets

This dashboard enables stakeholders to make data-driven decisions to improve operational efficiency, customer retention, and overall business performance.
