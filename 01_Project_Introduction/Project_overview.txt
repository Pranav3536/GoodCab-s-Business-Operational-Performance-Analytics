--> PROJECT SUMMARY

1. This project analyzes operational and passenger data of GoodCabs, a cab service company operating in tier-2 cities across India.
2. The analysis focuses on key business metrics such as total trips, revenue, passenger ratings, trip demand patterns, and passenger retention.
3. Multiple analytical dashboards were created to understand city-wise performance, demand distribution, and passenger behavior.
4. The project simulates a real-world business intelligence scenario where insights help the operations team improve service quality, optimize demand management, and achieve performance targets.
