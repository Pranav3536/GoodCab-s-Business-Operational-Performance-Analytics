📌 BUSINESS INSIGHTS

Revenue Concentration: 33% of total trip volume and revenue is driven by just two key hubs (Jaipur and Lucknow), indicating high geographic dependency.

Seasonal Demand Peaks: Trip volume and revenue peaked significantly in February, followed by a gradual decline, suggesting strong seasonality in travel patterns.

Retention Challenges: While repeat passengers are present, 74% of users are new, highlighting a critical need for loyalty programs to improve long-term retention.

Service Quality Gaps: Critical rating variances (e.g., Lucknow at 6.49) pinpoint specific regions where service quality is significantly below the target benchmarks.

Target Underperformance: Major cities like Lucknow and Vadodara missed trip targets by a wide margin, identifying clear supply-side or marketing bottlenecks.

Persona-Driven Demand: 85% of demand occurs on weekdays with a massive peak on Mondays, confirming that the service primarily caters to working professionals.
