📌 CONCLUSION

• The analysis of GoodCabs transportation data provides a comprehensive view of 
  operational performance across multiple tier-2 cities.

• The dashboard highlights key metrics such as total trips, revenue generation, 
  passenger ratings, and demand patterns across different cities and time periods.

• City-level performance analysis helps identify high-performing locations as well 
  as areas where operational improvements are required.

• Passenger behavior insights, including repeat passengers and ratings, provide valuable 
  indicators of customer satisfaction and service quality.

• Overall, the project demonstrates how data analytics and interactive dashboards can 
  help organizations monitor performance, identify trends, and support data-driven decision making.
